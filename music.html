My Music

A selection of music I've made

All the songs here are made in ableton live 11 intro with free instruments. (Headphones recommended!)

Feel free to use any music here for non-commercial purposes. credit would be appreciated :)

  • "Alive"

    This was created as an experiment with automating transitions between synth parameters.

  • "Stargazing"

    I made this as one of my first attempts to intigrate drums with my usual synths

  • "This Green World"

    This was created as an overworld theme for an imaginary game using only harps.

  • "Might of Sun"

    Originally made for a string quartet, it uses synths due to my lack of good string instruments

  • "Pixel Fights"

    A fast chipchune-esque song using the surgeXT plugin and stock instruments.

  • "Hidden"

    A chipchune-esque entry for the OST composing jam 7

  • "Chaos"

    One of my first songs, somewhat jazzy. (definitly not smooth jazz though)

  • "Inhabited Cave"

    A faster remaster of a song I made called 'cave' with a distorted synth.
